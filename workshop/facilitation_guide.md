# Facilitation Guide

## 70% pre-processing

Run deterministic checks, prepare issue log, draft canonical map, and identify decision bottlenecks before the live session.

## 30% live decision session

Use the live session for human decisions only: canonical events, owner assignment, migration priority, and what AI may safely answer.

## Anti-patterns

- Spending the workshop manually reading every event.
- Letting AI rewrite taxonomy without owners.
- Treating readiness score as a final truth.
